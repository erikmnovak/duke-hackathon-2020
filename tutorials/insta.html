What is Instagram

Instagram is an app and a website where people from around the world can upload pictures or short videos of anything. It was launched in 2010 and is used to keep up with your family, friends, celebrities, and anybody else who you desire. Your answers to the survey reveal to us that it's a good fit for you!

Creating an Instagram account

Greeted with this start page, you’ll want to either click on the ‘Sign up’ button at the bottom or, if you already have a Facebook account, click on the ‘Log in with Facebook’ button in the middle.

You’ll want to fill in the boxes shown above. In the “Mobile number or email” box you can choose whether to input your mobile number or your email. Next up you can fill out your full name. For the ‘Username’ box you will be choosing what you want people to identify you with on instagram, we recommend your username being just your last name and your first name. Then you can choose a password. Make sure the password is something you can remember, but not something that is too easy to guess. Once you’re done, click the big green ‘sign up’ button and you'll officially have an Instagram account!

Getting started with your new account

At the top right of the screen you’ll see the main buttons of Instagram. Clicking on the little home icon returns you to the main page where you can see your feed. The arrow icon will bring you to your direct messages, the compass icon takes you to your Instagram explore page, and the heart icon will take you to a summary of the activity on your account. The final circle on the right will be your profile and the image in the circle will look different for every Instagram user.
